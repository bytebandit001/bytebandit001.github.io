Frequently Asked Questions

The Dhyaan Meditation App provides guided meditation sessions aimed at improving mindfulness, relaxation, and overall well-being. It offers various techniques, customizable session lengths, and tools like binaural beats to enhance your meditation practice.

To start using the Dhyaan Meditation App, simply select your preferred meditation session duration and choose the type of music you want. No account or subscription is required to begin.

No prior experience is needed. The app caters to both beginners and experienced meditators with guided sessions and tutorials to help you progress at your own pace.

We offer four types of binaural beats designed to enhance different meditation goals. Choose relaxation music for stress relief, focus and concentration for cognitive enhancement, deep meditation for extended practice, or awareness and creativity for mindfulness and creative growth.

The app supports a wide range of session lengths, from brief 5-minute practices to longer, more in-depth sessions. Choose a duration that fits your schedule and needs.

Yes, the Dhyaan Meditation App is completely free to use with no advertisements, and it is open-source. Our goal is to promote mental and spiritual well-being through accessible meditation tools.

We do not collect any personal data or use cookies. Your privacy is important to us.
